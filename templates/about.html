Vision

To become the most trusted crime analytics platform for citizens, law enforcement, and policymakers.

Advanced Crime Prediction Platform

CrimeRateAI is an intelligent, data-driven solution designed to forecast crime trends across major Indian cities. Our platform combines historical crime data, population statistics, and advanced machine learning models to provide actionable insights about urban safety.

We serve researchers, policymakers, law enforcement agencies, and concerned citizens by transforming complex crime data into understandable predictions and visualizations, helping communities make informed decisions about safety and urban planning.

Our Scientific Methodology

Data Sources & Processing

Primary Sources

  • NCRB (National Crime Records Bureau) (2010-present)
  • Census of India population data
  • State police department reports

Data Processing

  • Automated data validation pipelines
  • Missing data imputation techniques
  • Feature engineering for temporal patterns

Modeling Approach

Time-Series Models

  • Prophet (Facebook's forecasting tool)
  • ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average)
  • Exponential Smoothing (ETS)

Machine Learning

  • Random Forest Regression
  • Gradient Boosting (XGBoost)
  • Neural Networks (LSTM)

Ensemble Approach

  • Model stacking for improved accuracy
  • Crime-type specific model selection
  • Dynamic weighting based on performance
